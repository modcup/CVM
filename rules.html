Rules


This is the party game in which players complete fill-in-the-blank statements using mature-content and politically incorrect phrases provided. In this game, in clockwise order, each player take turns in being the judge, choosing a random question card with fill-in-the-blank statement. The other players must choose one answer among the ten random answers they are provided. The judge choose the funniest answer: the player who provided the answer gain one point. Play as much as you want. When you are tired to play, the game is over and the player with the most points is the winner
